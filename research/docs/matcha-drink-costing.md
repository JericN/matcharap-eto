# Matcha Drink Costing — Metro Manila (2026)

**For:** BriarBear matcha pop-up / booth · **Compiled:** 2026-06-23 · **Revised:** 2026-06-23 (milk correction)
**Serving basis:** 1 × 16oz iced drink. **Currency:** PHP. Retail-sourced; wholesale cuts 15–30% more.

> ⚠️ **CORRECTION (this revision):** an earlier draft understated **milk by 10×** (used ~₱2/cup; correct is
> **₱120/L ÷ 5 = ~₱24 per 200 ml**). Milk is actually one of the biggest cost lines (~₱18–24/cup). All COGS
> below are corrected. Net effect: COGS rose ~₱18–20/drink, and realistic gross margins are **~62–73%**
> (not the 80% the buggy version implied). The website calculator uses the corrected per-ml math.

## 4. Three build tiers (extras = sweetener+ice+packaging)
- **A — Budget:** culinary matcha 2g (~₱4) · fresh dairy · house syrup · basic cup. Extras ≈ ₱8.
- **B — Standard (recommended):** quality matcha 3g (~₱15) · oat/barista milk · Monin syrup · dome-lid cup. Extras ≈ ₱10.5.
- **C — Premium:** ceremonial matcha 4g (~₱64) · barista milk · real ingredients · branded cup. Extras ≈ ₱13.
